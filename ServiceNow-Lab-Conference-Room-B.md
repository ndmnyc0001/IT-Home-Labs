## Lab: ServiceNow Incident - Conference Room B (MacBook AV Failure)

# 1. The Problem (Intake)
# The user reported that their MacBook Pro (M3) would not send a signal to the Sony 65" display in Conference Room B.

# Incident Number: INC0010003

# Priority: 4 - Low (Planning)

# 2. Troubleshooting & Investigation
# I documented my technical steps in the Work Notes to ensure a clear audit trail.

# Initial Actions: Swapped HDMI cables and tested different ports on the table hub.

# Advanced Diagnostics: Validated MacBook display settings, reset NVRAM, and verified the Sony display was on the correct input (HDMI 1).

# Root Cause Identified: Suspected a failing USB-C to HDMI adapter.
<img width="1280" height="398" alt="Screen Shot 2026-02-22 at 12 31 21 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7f9b070b-9e17-4e4a-a89a-718e931bcf96" />

# 3. Resolution
# Fix: Replaced the faulty USB-C adapter with a new StarTech unit.

# Verification: Signal reached the display successfully. Confirmed room is fully operational.
